About SPARK Institute

Empowering the next generation of data leaders through world-class education and research

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized leader in data science and applied statistics education, driving innovation and transforming lives through data-driven decision-making.

We envision a future where data literacy empowers individuals and institutions across Africa and beyond to solve real-world challenges with intelligence, ethics, and impact.

Our Mission

We are committed to excellence in education, research, and community engagement. Our mission is to:

  • Educate and train the next generation of data scientists with cutting-edge skills.
  • Conduct impactful research that addresses regional and global challenges.
  • Promote data literacy and democratize access to data science tools.
  • Foster collaboration between academia, industry, and government.
