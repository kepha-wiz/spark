Admissions Process

Welcome to the Spark Institute of Statistics and Data Science admissions page. Here you will find all the information you need to apply.

Steps to Apply

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Submit required documents (transcripts, letters of recommendation, etc.).
  3. Pay the application fee.
  4. Schedule an interview (if required).
  5. Receive your admission decision.

Application Deadlines

  • Fall Semester: June 1
  • Spring Semester: November 1
  • Summer Semester: March 1

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements?

Applicants must have a bachelor's degree or equivalent and meet specific program prerequisites.

Is there an application fee?

Yes, there is a non-refundable application fee of $50.

Can I apply for financial aid?

Yes, we encourage all students to apply for financial aid. Please visit our financial aid page for more information.
